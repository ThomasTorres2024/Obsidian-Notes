# Column Space Definition
The [[Column Space]] is the [[Span]] of the column vectors of some [[Matrix]] $S$. Let the set of vectors $S$ be the following over the field $\mathbb{F}$:
$$S=\{ v_{1},v_{2},\cdots, v_{n} \}$$
Then the [[Image (Abstract Algebra)]] of $S$ is also its column space and also its span: 
$$ \text{Im}(S) = \text{Col}(S)= \text{Span}(S)= \sum_{i=1}^n \alpha_{i} v_{i} : \alpha_{i} \in \mathbb{F} $$
