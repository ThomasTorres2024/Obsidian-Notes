# Normed Space
A space is normed if the following criteria are satisfied where $x,y \in \mathcal{V}$ (the space in question) and $\alpha$ is the associated [[Field]] with the [[Vector Space]]:

1. $\ | \vec{x} \| \geq 0$ $\forall \vec{x} \in \mathcal{V}$
2. $\| \vec{x} \| = 0 \Longleftrightarrow \vec{x}=\vec{0}_{v}$
3. $\| \alpha \cdot \vec{x}\|= |\alpha| \cdot \| \vec{x} \|$
4. $\|\vec{x} +\vec{y} \| \leq \| x || + \| y \|$ ([[Triangle Inequality]])
