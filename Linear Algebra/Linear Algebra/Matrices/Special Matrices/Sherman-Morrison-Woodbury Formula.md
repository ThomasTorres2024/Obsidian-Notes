# Sherman-Morrison-Woodbury Formula
For an invertible matrix, $A \in \mathbb{F}^{n \times n}$, and for the [[Rank]] $k$ matrices $u,v \in \mathbb{F}^{n \times k}$ we have that the [[Rank k Perturbation]] of $A$ is:
$$(A-uv^H)^{-1}=A^{-1}-A^{-1}u(I-v^HA^{-1}u)^{-1}v^HA^{-1}$$
