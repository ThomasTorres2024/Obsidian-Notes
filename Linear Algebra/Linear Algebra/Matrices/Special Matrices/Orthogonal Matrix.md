# Orthogonal Matrix
An [[Orthogonal Matrix]] is a [[Matrix]] $A$ where $A \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n }$ and $A^TA=AA^T=I_{n}$ which is to say the inverse matrix of $A$ is the Identity matrix. Secondly, we also have that any [[Orthogonal Matrix]] is a [[Hermitian]] Matrix.
