# Outer Product 
The [[Outer Product]] is the [[Matrix Multiplication]] of two vectors, $u,v \in \mathbb{F}^{n}$ where we compute:
$$uv^H \in \mathbb{F}^{n \times n}$$
If $u \neq 0, v\neq 0$ then the resulting [[Outer Product]] is the general form of any matrix that is [[Rank]] 1.
