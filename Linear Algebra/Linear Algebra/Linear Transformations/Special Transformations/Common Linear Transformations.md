The following are common [[Linear Transformation]]s  in linear algebra: 

The rotation matrix, [[Givens Matrix]], which rotates a vector in $\mathbb{R}^2$:

$$\begin{align}
\begin{pmatrix}
    cos(\phi) & -sin(\phi)\\
    sin(\phi) & cos(\phi)\\
    \end{pmatrix}&
    \cdot
\begin{pmatrix}
    a_{1} \\
    a_{2} \\
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}$$

# Reflection Matrix Across X Axis: 
$$\begin{align}
\begin{pmatrix}
    1 & 0\\
    0 & -1\\
    \end{pmatrix}&
    \cdot
\begin{pmatrix}
    a_{1} \\
    a_{2} \\
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}
$$
# Reflection Matrix Across Y Axis: 
$$\begin{align}
\begin{pmatrix}
    -1 & 0\\
    0 & 1\\
    \end{pmatrix}&
    \cdot
\begin{pmatrix}
    a_{1} \\
    a_{2} \\
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}$$

# Reflection Through $y=-x$: 
$$\begin{align}
\begin{pmatrix}
    0 & -1\\
    -1 & 0\\
    \end{pmatrix}&
    \cdot
\begin{pmatrix}
    a_{1} \\
    a_{2} \\
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}$$

# Reflection Through The Origin: 
$$ \begin{align}
\begin{pmatrix}
    -1 & 0\\
    0 & -1\\
    \end{pmatrix}&
    \cdot
\begin{pmatrix}
    a_{1} \\
    a_{2} \\
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}$$

# Scale Along X Axis:
$$ \begin{align}
\begin{pmatrix}
    k & 0\\
    0 & 1\\
    \end{pmatrix}&
    \cdot
\begin{pmatrix}
    a_{1} \\
    a_{2} \\
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}
$$

# Scale Along Y Axis:
$$\begin{align}
\begin{pmatrix}
    1 & 0\\
    0 & k\\
    \end{pmatrix}&
    \cdot
\begin{pmatrix}
    a_{1} \\
    a_{2} \\
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}
$$
# Horizontal Sheer :
$$\begin{align}
\begin{pmatrix}
    1 & k\\
    0 & 1\\
    \end{pmatrix}&
    \cdot
\begin{pmatrix}
    a_{1} \\
    a_{2} \\
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}
$$
# Vertical Sheer :
$$ \begin{align}
\begin{pmatrix}
    1 & 0\\
    k & 1\\
    \end{pmatrix}&
    \cdot
\begin{pmatrix}
    a_{1} \\
    a_{2} \\
\end{pmatrix}
\end{align}$$
