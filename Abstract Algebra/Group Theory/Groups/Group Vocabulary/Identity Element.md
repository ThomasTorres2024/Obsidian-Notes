# Identity Element
In a [[Group]] $G$, the [[Identity Element]] $e$, is an element such that under the [[Binary Operation]], $\forall a \in G$, we have:
$$a=a \circ e=e\circ a$$

In a [[Ring]] an [[Identity Element]] over addition is the same, but over multiplication we have some number $1$ such that:
$$a=a\cdot 1 = 1 \cdot a$$
