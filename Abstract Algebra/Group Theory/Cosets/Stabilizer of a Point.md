# Stabilizer of a Point

Let $G$ be a [[Permutation Group]] of [[Permutation]]s of a set $S$. For each $i \in S$, then:

$$\text{stab}_{G}(i)= \{ \phi \in G : \phi(i)=i \}$$

Which is called the __stabilizer__ of $i$ in $G$. Here we consider the set of [[Permutation]]s of $G$ is a [[Subgroup]] of [[Permutation Group]]s.
