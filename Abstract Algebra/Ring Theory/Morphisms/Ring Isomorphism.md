# Ring Isomorphism 
Let $\phi$ be a ring [[Homomorphism]] from [[Ring]]s $R\to S$ then the following mapping is an isomorphic and the following prroceeds:

$$\begin{align}
\frac{R}{\text{ker}(\phi)}\longrightarrow \phi(R) \\
r+\text{ker}(\phi) \mapsto \phi(r)
\end{align}$$
