# Unit (Ring Theory)
A [[Unit (Ring Theory)]] is some $\alpha \in R$ where $R$ is a ring such that $\exists! a^{-1}$ such that $a^{-1}$ is the multiplicative inverse of the element $\alpha$. If $R$ is a [[Field]] then it follows that $\forall a \in R$, every element other than the [[Identity Element]] under addition is a unit.
