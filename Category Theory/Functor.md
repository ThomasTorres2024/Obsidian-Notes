# Functor 
A [[Functor]] is a [[Morphism]] between Categories ([[Category]]). Consider the functor $f$ which maps categories $A\to B$. Every [[Object (Category Theory)]] in $A$ gets mapped to a category in $B$ and accordingly every morphism in $A$ gets mapped to a category in $B$.
