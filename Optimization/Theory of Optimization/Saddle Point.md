# Saddle Point 
A [[Saddle Point]] is some $x \in D$ where $D$ is the domain of a function $f : \mathbb{R}^n \to \mathbb{R}$ such that, $\nabla f(x)=\vec{0}$, but the [[Saddle Point]] is NOT a [[Local Minima]] and is NOT a [[Local Maxima]].
