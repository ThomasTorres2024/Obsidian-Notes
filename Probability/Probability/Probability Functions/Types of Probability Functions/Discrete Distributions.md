# Discrete Distribution
A [[Discrete Distributions]] has a [[Probability Mass Function]], PMF associated with the random variable $X$, denoted by:
$$\mathbb{P}[X=x]$$
The [[Cumulative Density Functions]] of a [[Discrete Distributions]] is $F(x)=\mathbb{P}[X \leq x]$.
